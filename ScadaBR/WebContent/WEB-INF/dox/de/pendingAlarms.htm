General

The Pending Alarms section displays all active or inactive alarms that are pending acknowledgment by the user. Alarms are an effective means of informing the user about events that need some kind of attention. If the Pending Alarms section is collapsed, click on the icon to expand it.

Acknowledging and silencing alarms

In the table of Pending Alarms it is possible to monitor all alarms that have not yet been acknowledged. To acknowledge an alarm individually, click on the icon in the respective row in the table. To silence (or unsilence) an alarm, click on the icon in the respective row of the table. If you want to mute or acknowledge all alarms at once, use the Acknowledge all and Silence all options.

Note: Once an alarm is acknowledged, it disappears from the Pending Alarms list. If you want to find a specific alarm after it has been acknowledged, use the Event Search section.
